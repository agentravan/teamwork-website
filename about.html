Who We Are

Bridging the gap between Complex Compliance and Seamless Technology.

Our Vision & Identity

TeamWork Solutions was founded with a singular vision: to be the operational backbone of Indian and Global enterprises. We identified a critical gap in the market—companies were struggling to manage their "Human Capital" because of disjointed systems. The technology didn't talk to the compliance team, and the recruitment team didn't talk to payroll.

We are a Full-Stack HR Partner. This means we don't just sell you softwares (Global HRX) and walk away. We stay. We manage your statutory filings, we handle your recruitment drives, and we deploy our own experts to run your payroll.

Our Field of Expertise

We operate deep within the HR Tech & Legal Compliance sector. Our expertise spans across:

  • SaaS Development: Building the next-gen HRMS.
  • Labour Law: Navigating PF, ESIC, and Factory Acts.
  • Financial Audit: Payroll structuring and tax planning.
  • Talent Acquisition: RPO for mass and niche hiring.

Our Partnership Approach

We work as an extension of your team. Our "Partners" are our clients who trust us with their most sensitive data—their employee records and financial liabilities. We enable our partners to be audit-ready 365 days a year, reducing their legal risk to near zero.
