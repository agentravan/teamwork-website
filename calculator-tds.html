Understanding TDS & Tax Calculations

Everything you need to know about Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) for FY 2025-26.

What is Tax Deducted at Source (TDS)?

Tax Deducted at Source (TDS) is a mechanism designed by the Indian government to collect tax at the point of payment itself. When you make certain payments such as salary, rent, professional fees, or contractor payments, the payer deducts a portion of the payment as tax before crediting the remainder to the recipient.

Why Use a TDS Calculator?

  • Accurate compliance with the Income Tax Act
  • Correct tax planning and withholding
  • Avoidance of penalties due to under-deduction
